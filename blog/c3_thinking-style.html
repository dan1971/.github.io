2/22/2015 - A window with a view...of my headspace :o

It has been an interesting breaking down and putting into words thinking style. I would say I agree with the estimation of my thinking style and it seems DBC is a perfect fit in many ways such as collaborative and community based.

The biggest struggles in Phase 0 so far have been related to keeping straight the multiple modes of communication and the weekly responsibilities to each mode. I also enjoy more in-person environments and am excited to get on-site so we can meet up and talk, collaborate and relate in-person.

I have found my focus has been so intense due to my passion for this process that it has hindered my creativity and narrowed my view with regards to problem solving. I need to relax my grip and allow my mind to wander out of the laser and look to the periphery for coding solutions.
